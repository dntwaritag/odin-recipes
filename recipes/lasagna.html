Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna

Description

Delicious layers of pasta, cheese, sauce, and meat, baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta sheets until al dente.
  2. Cook the ground beef and mix with tomato sauce.
  3. Layer pasta, ricotta, mozzarella, and meat sauce in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers and bake until cheese is melted and bubbly.
